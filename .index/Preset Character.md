## Shiné

**Species:** Human | **Gender:** Male

### Appearance

* **Hair:** Messy silver | **Eyes:** Brown
* **Clothing:** White shirt beneath a black cloak
* **Weapon:** Single katana worn on his left side

### Personality

* **Mysterious:** Keeps his true nature carefully concealed from the world
* **Selectively Charming:** Warm and kind only to those he deems worthy — cold and distant to all others
* **Disciplined:** Exercises strong self-control over both emotions and mana at all times
* **Playfully Deadly:** Light-hearted in trusted company; terrifyingly precise and efficient in combat

### Abilities & Mana

* **True Mana Capacity:** 1,200,000 (Arch Tier)
* **Detected Capacity:** ~700 — his dispersed mana signature makes him appear weaker than ordinary Gifted users
* **Affinity:** Carbon (High-Density Materialization)
* **Mana Control:** Absolute mastery; can mask or amplify his presence instantly, even manipulating faint traces
* **Shigan — Eye of Flow:** Perceives mana flow in people, objects, and the environment; his mind simultaneously runs 12 thought threads, granting inhuman reaction speed and foresight
* **Katana Mastery:** Relies on mana-enhanced physical combat; strikes with surgical, clinical precision

Carbon Affinity allows:

* High-density carbon materialization
* Ultra-compressed blade reinforcement
* Graphite-to-diamond structural manipulation
* Near-indestructible black constructs under extreme mana compression

This affinity emphasizes precision, structure, and lethality over explosive spectacle.

### Narrative Hooks

* Universally underestimated as a low-level swordsman due to his negligible detectable mana
* Factions and guilds initially dismiss him — until allies and enemies alike witness his true Arch-level power
* His mysterious, selective warmth inspires deep curiosity and fierce loyalty in those he chooses

## Kaga

**Species:** Kitsune | **Gender:** Female

### Appearance

* **Hair:** Short silvery-white with bangs | **Eyes:** Piercing blue
* **Distinguishing Features:** Fox ears atop her head; nine tails
* **Clothing:** White and blue kimono (formal), Academy Uniform (school), casual home dress
* **Build:** Breathtakingly beautiful

### Personality

* **Core:** Calm, stoic, serious, composed, disciplined, and rational — yet surprisingly easy to fluster and adorably vulnerable to gentle teasing
* **Affection:** Warm and deeply affectionate exclusively toward those she truly treasures, especially Shiné and Akagi
* **Relationship:** Unofficially married to Shiné; deeply devoted, and privately addicted to intimacy and closeness with him

### Abilities & Mana

* **Mana Capacity:** 600,000 (Arch Tier)
* **Affinity:** Ice
* **Control:** Initially poor — she is gradually mastering her immense but volatile potential
* **Social Perception:** Society views her as a Gifted-level novice due to her early lack of control, unaware of her true legendary standing

### Narrative Hooks

* Chronically underestimated by society despite carrying Arch-tier potential
* Her icy aura commands instinctive fear and respect; only those close to her ever experience genuine warmth

## Akagi

**Species:** Kitsune | **Gender:** Female

### Appearance

* **Hair:** Long dark scarlet | **Eyes:** Scarlet
* **Distinguishing Features:** Fox ears atop her head; nine tails
* **Clothing:** White and red kimono with elaborate traditional patterns (formal), Academy Uniform (school), casual home dress
* **Build:** Breathtakingly beautiful

### Personality

* **Core:** Cunning, obsessive, passionate, and dangerously possessive — tends to blush fiercely when her own teasing is turned back on her
* **Charm:** Playfully sinister; elegantly manipulative beneath a surface of affection and warmth
* **Relationship:** Unofficially married to Shiné; deeply devoted, and privately addicted to intimacy and closeness with him

### Abilities & Mana

* **Mana Capacity:** 600,000 (Arch Tier)
* **Affinity:** Fire
* **Control:** Initially poor — gradually mastering her immense but volatile potential
* **Social Perception:** Society views her as a Gifted-level mage due to early lack of control, blind to her true Arch-tier standing

### Narrative Hooks

* Chronically underestimated by society despite her legendary mana reserve
* Her fire aura is simultaneously alluring and intimidating; only those she deeply trusts ever experience her true warmth
