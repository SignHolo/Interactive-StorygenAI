# WORLD SETTING

Leisen is a vast, bustling metropolis where magic is not a rarity but a necessity—woven into every facet of daily life. 
Nearly every person possesses mana, though their capacity and mastery vary greatly.
The city thrives on trade, adventuring guilds, and the everyday application of magic. 
Beneath its prosperous facade, however, lie layers of corruption, crime, and political intrigue. 
Those blessed with greater mana capacity often consider themselves superior, giving rise to subtle yet enduring social divides.

Every individual possesses mana through a personal Mana Core.
However, not all become mages.

Mana can be applied through multiple paths:

* Spellcasting (Mage Path)
* Weapon Enchantment (Knight / Swordsman Path)
* Physical Reinforcement (Body Enhancement Path)
* Craft Enhancement (Artisan Path)
* Tactical Sensing (Scout / Assassin Path)

Mages dominate in versatility and range, but reinforcement specialists remain equally lethal in close combat.

# MANA SYSTEM

## Mana Capacity Tiers

Ordinary Citizens

* 500–10,000 Mana
* 80% of population

Gifted

* 20,000–95,000 Mana
* 20% of population

Arch

* 100,000+ Mana
* Ratio: 1 in 10 thousands.

Mana capacity determines output potential but does not determine Affinity.

# MANA AFFINITY SYSTEM

## Core Rules

* Every individual is born with 1 Natural Affinity
* 2 Affinities are considered rare
* 3 or more are exceptional anomalies

Affinity affects:

* Spell efficiency
* Reinforcement compatibility
* Learning speed
* Mana behavior

Affinity does not determine total mana capacity.

---

## Affinity Categories

### Basic Elemental (Common)

* Fire
* Water
* Wind
* Earth

Stable and widely taught.

---

### Advanced Elemental (Independent but Related)

Advanced affinities are standalone types but remain elementally related to Basic types.

Examples:

* Ice (related to Water)
* Lightning (related to Wind)
* Metal (related to Earth)
* Magma (related to Fire and Earth)
* Plasma

Efficiency Rule:

* Natural Affinity: maximum efficiency
* Related Element: reduced efficiency
* Unrelated Element: very low efficiency

Advanced affinities are not evolutions of Basic types.

---

### Unique Affinity (Rare)

Usually genetic, occasionally random manifestation.

Examples include:

* Gravity
* Shadow
* Sound
* Blood
* Light
* Space
* Binding
* Decay

Rarity hierarchy:

Common Elemental > Advanced Elemental > Unique

No affinity is forbidden. Rarity alone defines prestige and expectation.

# MANA CORE SYSTEM

## Mana Core

Every individual possesses a Mana Core, the source of their mana.

Each Mana Core has a unique frequency signature.
No two cores share identical frequency.

The Core continuously emits mana outward in the form of an aura.

---

## Core Frequency

Core frequency affects:

* Spell stability
* Affinity resonance
* Combat interference
* Mana flow behavior

High-frequency cores:

* Faster mana output
* Higher volatility

Low-frequency cores:

* Greater stability
* Higher endurance

---

# MANA AURA AND SPELL FORMATION

The Mana Core emits mana as a natural aura surrounding the body.

This aura:

* Extends to a limited radius
* Acts as the medium through which spells are formed

Key Rule:

Spells can only be formed within the user’s aura radius.

If mana cannot reach a location, it cannot be shaped there.

---

## Range Mechanics

Formation Radius
The maximum distance from the body where mana can be shaped into a spell.

Influenced by:

* Core strength
* Control mastery
* Affinity stability

Effect Range
Once formed, spells may travel beyond the aura.

However, the further a spell moves away from the caster’s frequency field, the more unstable it becomes.

This creates natural combat limitations.

# EDUCATION SYSTEM

## Mana Academies

Mana Academies are the foundation of elite development in the realm.

All individuals with sufficient Mana Core stability may enroll.

Academies teach:

* Mana Core control
* Aura expansion and compression
* Affinity mastery
* Spell formation theory
* Physical reinforcement systems
* Combat applications
* Mana ethics and law

Academies function as both educational institutions and social filters.

---

# HEIZEN MANA ACADEMY

## Overview

Heizen Mana Academy is one of the most prestigious institutions in the kingdom.

It is known for:

* Advanced Core Frequency research
* High number of Arch-tier alumni
* Strict internal ranking systems

Heizen produces:

* Elite mages
* Knight captains
* High-ranking guild operatives
* Political advisors

Admission is competitive and reputation-driven.

---

## Structure

Enrollment Age: 18–20
Graduation Age: 24–26
Duration: 6 years

### Academic Stages

Foundation Stage

* Core stabilization
* Aura control fundamentals
* Affinity assessment

Mastery Stage

* Path specialization (Mage or Reinforcement focus)
* Efficiency optimization
* Combat simulations

Specialization Stage

* Advanced Affinity techniques
* Tactical coordination training
* Guild or military placement trials

---

## Internal Culture

* Students are ranked based on performance and mana efficiency
* Affinity rarity shapes early expectations
* Multi-affinity students attract institutional interest
* Arch-level potential individuals are discreetly monitored

Rivalries formed within Heizen often influence future political and military hierarchies in the realm.
