### LORE/BACKGROUND ###

LEISEN:
  Overview:
    Description: >
      Leisen is a vast, bustling metropolis where magic is not a rarity but a necessity—woven into every facet of daily life. 
      Nearly every person possesses mana, though their capacity and mastery vary greatly. 
      The city thrives on trade, adventuring guilds, and the everyday application of magic. 
      Beneath its prosperous facade, however, lie layers of corruption, crime, and political intrigue. 
      Those blessed with greater mana capacity often consider themselves superior, giving rise to subtle yet enduring social divides.

  Mana_System:
    Tiers:
      Ordinary_Citizens:
        Mana_Range: 500–10,000
        Population_Percentage: 80
        Description: >
          The majority of Leisen’s population. Their mana suffices for daily conveniences, minor enchantments, 
          and basic combat or defensive spells. Most live practical, non-adventuring lives.
      Gifted:
        Mana_Range: 20,000–95,000
        Population_Percentage: 20
        Description: >
          Skilled individuals with significantly higher mana reserves. They form the backbone of Leisen’s 
          adventurers, mercenaries, and royal guard. Many are recruited into guilds or serve in the city’s 
          military and research institutions.
      Arch:
        Mana_Range: 100,000+
        Population_Ratio: 1_in_10_million
        Description: >
          Beings of near-mythical power capable of altering the course of history. Their appearances are rare, 
          and their true numbers uncertain. Some doubt they exist at all, while others worship them as living legends.

  Society_and_Culture:
    Guilds:
      Role: >
        Guilds dominate the social and economic life of Leisen, handling everything from monster extermination 
        and trade security to magical research and artifact recovery.
      Notes: >
        Most Gifted individuals find their calling within guilds, whose ranks and reputations shape the city’s hierarchy.
    Races:
      Humans:
        Traits: "Versatile and adaptable, capable of thriving in nearly any profession or environment."
      Elves:
        Traits: "Long-lived and naturally attuned to mana; their magic tends to be precise and elegant."
      Beastmen:
        Traits: "Humanoid beings with animal features—ears, tails, and heightened senses—known for their agility and instinct."
    Cultural_Notes:
      - "Mana is both a practical tool and a measure of personal worth."
      - "Societal status often correlates with mana capacity, though mastery and creativity are equally respected."
      - "Magical education is widespread, but resources and opportunities remain unevenly distributed."

  Education_System:
    Mana_Academies:
      Overview: >
        Mana Academies serve as the cornerstone of Leisen’s youth development. Nearly every child showing 
        potential for mana control is enrolled, regardless of lineage. Within their halls, students hone their 
        magical abilities, study theory, and learn about mana ethics and history. The academies act as both 
        educational institutions and social filters—where future leaders, adventurers, and innovators are forged.
      Structure:
        Enrollment_Age: 12–14
        Graduation_Age: 18–20
        Duration: "Typically 6 years, divided into three stages: Foundation, Mastery, and Specialization."
      Curriculum:
        Core_Subjects:
          - Mana Theory and Application
          - Combat Magic and Self-Defense
          - Mana Flow Control and Efficiency
          - Magical Ethics and Law
          - Elemental Studies
          - History of Mana and Civilization
        Electives:
          - Alchemy and Enchantment
          - Healing and Restoration Arts
          - Magical Engineering
          - Summoning Practices
          - Strategic Tactics and Battlefield Mana
      Post_Graduation_Paths:
        Adventurer:
          Description: >
            Graduates who pursue freedom, fame, and fortune through exploration, monster hunting, and questing. 
            Most join adventuring guilds or form independent parties.
        Researcher:
          Description: >
            Scholars devoted to advancing magical knowledge—working in laboratories, academies, or private guilds 
            to study artifacts, mana theory, or new spellcraft.
        Knight_or_Royal_Guard:
          Description: >
            Elite combatants trained to protect Leisen’s royalty and uphold national defense. 
            Often the pride of the academy’s martial division.
        Politician_or_Administrator:
          Description: >
            Graduates skilled in leadership, diplomacy, and governance. They typically pursue positions in 
            Leisen’s bureaucracy or manage regional affairs.
        Artisan_or_Engineer:
          Description: >
            Innovators who combine magic with craftsmanship—creating enchanted tools, mana-based machinery, 
            and infrastructure that sustain the city.
      Cultural_Impact: >
        Graduating from a Mana Academy is a symbol of prestige and social mobility. 
        The most renowned academies are fiercely competitive, and the bonds—or rivalries—formed within 
        their walls often shape the city’s future power dynamics.
